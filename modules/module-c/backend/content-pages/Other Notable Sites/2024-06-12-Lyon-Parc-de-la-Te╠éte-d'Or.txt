Hey nature lovers and city explorers! Escape the urban hustle and bustle at Parc de la Tête d'Or, Lyon's magnificent urban oasis.  This sprawling green space, one of the largest in Europe, offers a tranquil escape amidst the city's vibrant energy.

Take a leisurely stroll through the lush gardens, admire the vibrant flowerbeds, and discover hidden corners filled with peace and serenity.  Explore the captivating botanical gardens, home to a diverse collection of plant species from around the world.

For animal lovers, a visit to the zoo is a must-see!  Encounter exotic creatures from across the globe, learn about conservation efforts, and witness the beauty of wildlife up close.

Don't miss the opportunity to rent a boat and glide across the serene lake, enjoying breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.
