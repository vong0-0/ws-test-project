Lyon Musee Miniature et Cinéma

Hey movie buffs, art enthusiasts, and anyone with a love for the miniature! Prepare to be amazed by the Musée Miniature et Cinéma, a unique museum that blends art, history, and cinematic magic.

This captivating venue showcases an incredible collection of miniature models, painstakingly crafted replicas of famous buildings, iconic film sets, and charming scenes from everyday life.

Here's why a visit to the Musée Miniature et Cinéma is a must-do:

  • A Feast for the Eyes: Gaze in awe at the incredible detail and artistry of each miniature creation. From bustling cityscapes to tranquil countryside scenes, every model is a testament to the dedication and skill of the creators.
  • A Glimpse into History: Explore meticulously recreated historical settings, from ancient Roman cities to medieval castles, providing a fascinating window into the past.
  • Cinema Magic: Step onto miniature movie sets and discover the artistry behind creating cinematic worlds. Experience the magic of Hollywood through the lens of these fascinating recreations.
  • Interactive Exhibits: Engage with hands-on activities and learn about the techniques used to create miniatures, adding a playful and educational dimension to your visit.

Highlights to Discover:

  • The Diorama of Lyon: A stunning recreation of Lyon's historic center, showcasing the city's iconic landmarks in intricate detail.
  • The Miniature World: A whimsical journey through diverse settings, including a bustling Parisian street, a charming English village, and a spooky haunted house.
  • The Movie Set of "La Vie en Rose": Relive the magic of the acclaimed film "La Vie en Rose" by exploring the meticulously recreated film set.

Don't Miss:

  • Special Exhibitions: The museum frequently hosts temporary exhibitions featuring different themes and artists, providing a constantly evolving experience.
  • The Gift Shop: Take home a miniature souvenir to remember your visit to this enchanting museum.

Ready to step into a world of wonder? The Musée Miniature et Cinéma awaits!
